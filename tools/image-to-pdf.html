About the Image to PDF Converter

This tool gives you full control over how your images (JPG or PNG) are converted into a PDF. All processing happens 100% in your browser, ensuring your files remain private.

Advanced Conversion Options

  • Page Size (Fit to Image): The default and most popular option. This creates a PDF page that is the exact size of your image. This is perfect for high-resolution photos or scanned receipts where you don't want any scaling.
  • Page Size (A4/Letter): This will create a standard-sized PDF page and scale your image down to fit, respecting the margins you select.
  • Auto Orientation: When using A4 or Letter, this feature will check if your image is wide (Landscape) or tall (Portrait) and automatically rotate the PDF page to match it.
  • Margin Control: Add a 'Small' (0.5 inch) or 'Large' (1 inch) border around your images on A4/Letter pages, or. select 'No Margin' to have them fill the space (bleed).

How to Use This Tool

  1. Add Images: Drag and drop your JPG and PNG files, or click "Select Files". You can add multiple files at once.
  2. Set Options: Choose your preferred Page Size, Orientation, and Margin for the final PDF.
  3. Reorder: Use the "Move Up" and "Move Down" arrows on each file card to arrange them in the order you want them to appear in the PDF.
  4. Create & Download: Click the "Create PDF" button. The tool will process your images and download the finished, combined PDF.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is this Image to PDF converter secure?

Yes, it is 100% secure. This tool runs entirely in your web browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring your data remains completely private.

What image formats can I upload?

You can upload files in JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg) and PNG (.png) formats. You can upload multiple files at once and combine both formats into a single PDF.

What do the 'Page Size' options mean?

'A4' and 'Letter' will create a standard-sized PDF page and scale your image to fit. 'Fit to Image' (default) is special: it creates a PDF page that is the exact size of your image, which is perfect for receipts or non-standard documents.

How does 'Auto' orientation work?

When using 'A4' or 'Letter' page sizes, 'Auto' will intelligently check the dimensions of each image. If an image is wider than it is tall, it will be placed on a Landscape page. If it is taller than it is wide, it will be placed on a Portrait page.
